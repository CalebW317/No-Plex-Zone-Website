Best Devices for Plex

Plex is incredibly versatile and runs on almost any device. However, for the best streaming experience, I recommend the following. Click the "View on Amazon" button to learn more or purchase!

NVIDIA Shield TV

Often considered the best all-around Plex client due to its powerful processor, 4K HDR support, and excellent codec compatibility. Ideal for serious streamers.

View on Amazon

Apple TV 4K

A premium option with a sleek interface, robust performance, and excellent integration within the Apple ecosystem. Perfect for faithful Apple users.

View on Amazon

Roku Devices

Affordable, user-friendly, and widely compatible. Roku devices offer a great balance of performance and value, especially models like the Roku Ultra.

View on Amazon

Smart TVs (Built-in Plex)

Many modern Samsung, LG, Sony, and other smart TVs have a built-in Plex app, offering convenience without needing an extra device. Check your TV's app store.

View on Amazon

Mobile Devices

Perfect for on-the-go streaming. Both iOS and Android devices have excellent Plex apps that recently went under a complete overhaul.

View on Amazon

Google TV Streamer 4K

A smart and affordable streaming dongle with a remote, integrating Google TV for a streamlined content discovery experience. Great for Android users.
